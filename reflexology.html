What is Reflexology?

Reflexology is a non-invasive health therapy that can be effective in promoting healing and well-being. The origins of reflexology can be traced back around 5000 years to Egypt and China. We know that reflexology was part of Egyptian society as hieroglyphics dating back to 2330 BC were found in an Egyptian pyramid.

Reflexology is much broader than a foot massage, despite its appearance. Based on the notion of Qi (pronounced "chee"), the body's various pathways and zones are connected by an invisible life force. According to this notion, if your Qi is disturbed or blocked due to stress, injury, or sickness, your body will become unbalanced, unable to cure itself, and pain may start to appear. Reflexologists seek to improve the flow of energy by stimulating the corresponding pressure points in the hands and feet to restore balance and the flow of Qi. This allows for a deep state of relaxation that encourages the body to heal itself.
