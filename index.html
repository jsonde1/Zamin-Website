Reflexology is a non invasive health therapy that can help be effective in promoting healing and well being. The origins of reflexology can be traced back around 5000 years to Egypt and China. We know that reflexology was part of Egyptian society as hieroglyphics dating back to 2330BC were found in an egyptian pyramid.

What is Reflexology?

Reflexology is much broader than a foot massage, despite its appearance. Based on the notion of Qi, (pronounced "chee") the body's various pathways and zones are connected by an invisible life force. According to the notion, if your Qi is disturbed or blocked due to stress, injury, or sickness your body will become unbalanced, unable to cure itself, and pain may start to appear. Reflexologist seek to improve the flow of energy by stimulating the corresponding pressure point in the hands and feet to restore balance and flow of Qi. This allows a deep state of relaxation which encourages the body to heal itself.
