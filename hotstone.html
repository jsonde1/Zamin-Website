Frequently Asked Questions

Once you arrive at Radiance, your therapist will greet you and ask you some questions about your reasons for taking a massage, whilst you will fill out a Massage Consultation Form (a quick survey where you record any important medical history, pain areas, injuries and any other physical conditions we should know about). Most importantly, when your massage therapist reviews the Massage Consultation form with you, they will be quickly able to: Ensure you are safe and healthy to receive massage. Understand what you want from the session, and also your preferences on the level of pressure you would like during your massage. If you don’t know, they will advise, it’s their job! Identify any specific areas of your body in pain or needing specific attention during your massage, whilst also checking any areas you do not want massaged.

Your massage therapist will take you to a quiet, comfortable treatment room where a padded massage bed and clean sheets will have been prepared. The Massage Therapist will tell you what position to lie in on the table, and which sheets to cover yourself with, before they then leave the room and wait outside to allow you to undress to your own personal comfort.

You will always be covered with the top sheet during your massage session. The process your massage therapist will follow is to only uncover the part of the body they are working on, ensuring that your modesty is 100% respected 100% of the time.

Once ur treatment is finished, your therapist will leave the room to let you re dress and wait outside the room with a glass of water. Our treatments range from 30 minutes to 90 minutes. Each treatment will require clients to complete a consultation form prior to any treatment. We look forward to seeing you! If you have any questions about the etiquette during a massage, one of our therapists will be happy to answer all your queries in as much detail or privacy as you require, either on the phone or in person
